# Data Collected by [Get Community](https://www.getcommunity.com/)

Ask:

- What personal data is collected? (email, IP, name, behavior)
- Where is it stored?
- Who is it shared with?

Look for:

- Forms (contact, signup, checkout)
- Analytics (Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, etc.)
- Cookies and tracking scripts
- Embedded third-party tools (chat widgets, videos, maps)
- Account systems (logins, user profiles)
